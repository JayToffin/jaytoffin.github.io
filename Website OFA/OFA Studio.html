Design Philosophy

Less, but better.  Nothing is superfluous. Everything is clear, legible, and designed to enhance the user experience.

A tribute to the timeless design of Dieter Rams for Braun — reinterpreted through a contemporary language while remaining faithful to the essential.

Built entirely from steel for lasting durability. Controls kept to the essentials, made iconic by orange — an explicit nod to historic Braun devices. The analog gauge and ribbed orange side panels add character without ever being intrusive.  More than a coffee machine: a declaration of design intent. An object made to last, made to simplify the everyday ritual of brewing coffee.

Unique Design, Dual Soul.
