What is Click Day?

Click Day is a playful monthly ritual for coffee professionals — a digital race where companies compete to be the fastest to enter their code and secure a €1 espresso machine.

01
A monthly digital race
Once a month, Click Day is triggered at a surprise moment. Only those who have registered can take part in the race.
02
For coffee professionals
Distributors, importers, roasteries, and local dealers can register their company and join a curated, up-to-date registry.
03
A symbolic €1 prize
The first company to complete the Click Day flow earns the right to purchase a professional espresso machine for just €1.
Who can join Click Day?

Click Day is designed to be global, but some markets may not be able to join from day one. Participation depends on strategic and regulatory constraints.

Due to strategic and regulatory constraints, some geographic markets may not be eligible to register for Click Day. Companies operating in restricted markets will not be able to complete the registration process, and will be informed during onboarding.

Our goal is to expand participation over time while respecting local rules and maintaining a fair, consistent experience for all companies.
