Toffin BootCamp (TBC) 2024 is a high-quality event focused on certain topics, with international collaboration and experts participation. This workshop aims to set standards for excellence while also providing possibilities for information sharing, business, and professional growth. We provide emergent learning experiences with measurable business and skill outcomes.

These workshops are known for their exceptional quality in terms of content, teaching methodologies, and participant engagement. We are designed to provide attendees with in-depth knowledge, advanced skills, and practice in a particular subject area or field.

A world-class workshop offers several key benefits that can significantly impact personal and professional development:

Access to Expertise
Participants learn from top industry professionals, gaining insights and best practices from their experience and knowledge.
Networking Opportunities
Workshops attract like-minded individuals from around the world, creating opportunities to build valuable connections and collaborate on future projects.⁠
Hone Skills
World-class workshops often provide practical, real-world exercises that allow participants to apply knowledge in real time, improving retention and skill development.
Exposure to Cutting-Edge Techniques
Participants are exposed to the latest industry trends, tools, and technologies, helping them stay ahead in their field.
Boosted Credibility
Attending and completing a recognized world-class workshop can enhance your resume or portfolio, adding prestige to your qualifications.
Inspiration and Motivations
Being surrounded by top-tier professionals and peers can reignite passion and motivation for your work or craft.
Improved Problem-Solving Skills
With a focus on advanced techniques, participants often leave with enhanced problem-solving abilities, applicable across a variety of situations.

Irene Wahyuni Hartono or familiarly called Icen, was Wajah Femina Finalist in 2008 and Editorial Photographer from 2007 to 2019. She started to kitchen experiment when moved to Tulungagung. Icen loved to sharing recipe and baking processing on social media and known as Kitchen Experimentalist and being more known especially when pandemic hits. At the present time she is more serious and dedicating to sharing her experiment by being workshop speaker.
