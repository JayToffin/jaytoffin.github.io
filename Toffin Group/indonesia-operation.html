PT Toffin Indonesia

PT Toffin Indonesia serves as the core operating entity of Toffin Group and is one of the established distributors of coffee machines, F&B equipment, and premium beverage ingredients in Indonesia.


Focused on the HORECA (hotel, restaurant, café) sector, the company supports a broad and diverse customer base, ranging from independent operators to established hospitality groups and national chains. Over time, PT Toffin Indonesia has built a reputation as a reliable and long-term partner within the industry.


With a portfolio of more than 1,000 SKUs across both international and local premium brands, the company offers a comprehensive range of products that cater to various operational needs within the beverage and hospitality space. This includes coffee machines across multiple segments, beverage ingredients such as syrups and powders, as well as supporting equipment essential for day-to-day operations.


In addition to product distribution, PT Toffin Indonesia provides integrated solutions designed to support customers throughout their business journey. These services include concept consultation, equipment selection and installation, barista training, and after-sales service supported by a nationwide technical network.


Through this approach, PT Toffin Indonesia aims to support its partners in maintaining operational consistency, improving efficiency, and delivering quality experiences to their end customers.

PT Toffin Digital

PT Toffin Digital was established to evolve our customer interactions from primarily physical engagement to an integrated digital environment. With a broad product portfolio and an extensive customer base, we identified the need for a platform that enables customers to seamlessly explore Toffin’s offerings, allowing us to better understand customer behavior and preferences at scale.


Over time, PT Toffin Digital has developed into the Group’s technology and innovation hub. It is responsible for building and managing:


  • Proprietary ERP systems
  • Customer-facing mobile applications
  • AI-enabled tools to support business operations and decision-making

Through these capabilities, PT Toffin Digital plays a central role in strengthening operational efficiency, enhancing customer experience, and supporting Toffin Group’s evolution into a more integrated, technology-enabled organization.

PT Toffin Casa Indonesia

PT Toffin Casa Indonesia represents Toffin Group’s strategic expansion into the premium lifestyle and consumer segment, extending our presence beyond professional hospitality into the modern home.


The company focuses on the curation and distribution of high-end home and lifestyle brands that integrate design excellence, advanced engineering, and functional performance. By bringing globally recognized brands into the Indonesian market, Casa serves a growing segment of consumers who value both aesthetics and quality in everyday living.


Our portfolio includes internationally renowned partners such as:


  • SMEG — an iconic Italian brand known for its distinctive design language and heritage-driven craftsmanship
  • Rocket Espresso — manufacturer of hand-built espresso machines that embody precision, performance, and artisanal tradition
  • V-ZUG — Switzerland’s leading luxury appliance brand, recognized for its commitment to innovation, sustainability, and minimalist design
  • Bang & Olufsen — a globally respected name in high-end audio, combining engineering excellence with refined industrial design

Beyond product distribution, PT Toffin Casa Indonesia plays a broader role in shaping how these products are positioned and experienced within the Indonesian market. The company emphasizes a lifestyle-driven approach—where coffee, design, and technology are integrated into cohesive living environments.


Through curated brand selection, retail experience development, and customer engagement, Casa aims to bridge the gap between professional-grade performance and residential application, elevate the standard of living by transforming everyday spaces into thoughtfully designed environments—where functionality, aesthetics, and experience coexist seamlessly.
