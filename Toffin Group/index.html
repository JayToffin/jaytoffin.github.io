About Toffin Group

Toffin Group is an integrated coffee and beverage solutions company built at the intersection of distribution, technology, and lifestyle.


Founded in 2007, Toffin began with a simple mission: to introduce high-quality beverage ingredients to Indonesia during the early rise of the coffee shop industry. What started as a small operation has since evolved into a multi-entity group operating across Southeast Asia and Europe, serving thousands of businesses in the hospitality and beverage sector.


Today, Toffin Group operates through a comprehensive ecosystem that spans B2B distribution, digital platforms, lifestyle retail, manufacturing, and F&B operations.

What We Do

Core Business

B2B Distribution

Toffin is one of the most established players in Indonesia’s coffee and beverage industry, with a dominant presence in coffee machines, ingredients, and supporting equipment.

We provide:

  • Multi-brand coffee machines across all market segments
  • Beverage ingredients including syrups, powders, and specialty products
  • End-to-end café and restaurant solutions
  • Nationwide after-sales service infrastructure with extensive technical coverage
Innovation

Digital & Technology

Through Toffin Digital, we are building a data-driven layer on top of the beverage industry.

Our platforms enable:

  • Seamless interaction between Toffin and its customers
  • Behavioral insights to better understand and serve business needs
  • AI-powered tools to support customer growth and decision-making

We believe the future of distribution is not just physical—it is intelligent, connected, and adaptive.

Lifestyle

Lifestyle & Consumer

Toffin Casa represents our expansion into premium lifestyle and design-led products.

Curating globally recognized brands such as SMEG, Bang & Olufsen, V-ZUG, and Rocket Espresso, Casa positions Toffin within the modern consumer lifestyle—where coffee, design, and technology converge.
