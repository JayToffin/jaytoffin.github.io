Italian craftsmanship and innovation come together in espresso machine.

Officine Allegra is not a factory. It is a workshop — a place where craftsmanship is measured by patience, and every machine is built by hands that understand tradition. Officine Allegra brings together decades of experience in Italian espresso engineering with a vision for the future of coffee craftsmanship.

We build in small numbers, with the same philosophy that has shaped Italy’s finest workshops: precision, reliability, and timeless design.

Every Officine Allegra machine is made with care, tested without compromise, and crafted for those who believe that true quality is never rushed.

DINAMICA

At Officine Allegra, we believe great machines should feel invisible in use — intuitive, reliable, and designed around the coffee itself.

Dinamica is born from this philosophy: professional performance without unnecessary complexity. Built to last. Designed to inspire.

LAMINA

This is LAMINA—essential, precise, and uncompromising. A machine crafted for baristas who value clarity in every detail. Available in one-group or two-group configurations, Lamina adapts to your workflow, delivering flawless results with every pull.

Behind its clean lines and bold silhouette lies high-performance craftsmanship. From its durable build to its intelligent design, Lamina brings together power, elegance, and adaptability to redefine what coffee equipment should be.

Lamina isn’t here to follow. It’s built to lead.
