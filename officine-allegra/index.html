THE DINAMICA

Espresso machine by Officine Allegra is a perfect blend of Italian craftsmanship, innovation, and performance. Designed for professionals who demand precision and reliability, DINAMICA ensures every espresso shot is brewed to perfection.

Crafted in Italy, this machine features a high-capacity 11.4-liter boiler, ensuring consistent temperature control and steam pressure. With its dual-group technology, baristas can prepare multiple espressos simultaneously, making it ideal for high-volume environments.

Powered by 3000W / 4000W, the DINAMICA operates efficiently with a 230-240V 50Hz system, delivering optimal performance. Its ergonomic design, premium materials, and customizable color options make it both a functional and stylish addition to any café or coffee bar.

Experience the art of espresso with DINAMICA – where tradition meets modern innovation.
