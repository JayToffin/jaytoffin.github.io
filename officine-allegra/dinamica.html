MODEL DINAMICA

At Officine Allegra, we see design not as decoration, but as structure. In Dinamica, parallel lines create flow, essential volumes create space, and craftsmanship creates meaning. Technology is hidden inside beauty; beauty is rooted in functionality.

This is not just our first café espresso machine — it is the purest expression of our belief that engineering and aesthetics must move together, in perfect balance.

Features

Thermal Stability — Engineered for Excellence

Every shot starts with unwavering thermal precision, delivering consistent extraction without fluctuation.

Precision Steam with Quick-Opening Valve

A fast-response steam system designed for effortless milk texturing, even in high-demand environments.

Cool-Touch Steam Wand

Comfort and safety, with high-performance steam power — crafted for continuous use without overheating.

Espresso Platform and Americano Height

Designed with dual service heights to accommodate everything from ristretto cups to tall Americanos, effortlessly.

Adjustable Foot

Perfect stability on any surface, with automatic leveling to ensure seamless workflow setup.
