Officine Allegra is where legacy meets ambition — an artisan espresso machine company born in Milan, the heart of Italian design and coffee culture.

Step inside our workshop and it feels like stepping into another time. The walls carry the quiet stories of Italian craftsmanship, while the tools and hands at work bring those stories to life. Every machine we build is a dialogue between past and present — where timeless design meets precision engineering, and heritage finds its place in a modern world.

We produce in small batches, not to chase exclusivity, but to protect what matters most: machines built with intention, built to last, and built to inspire. For cafés and coffee professionals who see espresso as more than a drink — as a craft, a ritual, a part of life — Officine Allegra delivers a piece of Milanese soul in every cup.
