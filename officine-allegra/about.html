Officine Allegra is where legacy meets ambition—an artisan espresso machine company born from the collaboration between two generations of Italian craftsmanship.

At one end, Ennio Berti—a master builder with over 30 years of experience shaping the coffee industry. At the other, Alex Nuvoli—a young innovator with a decade of experience across leading coffee machine companies. Together, they’ve united tradition and vision to create something bold and personal: a new chapter in Italian espresso culture.

Officine Allegra is their shared dream—a workshop where every machine is handcrafted in Italy, engineered with soul, and built to honor the heritage of espresso. Here, classic Italian design is reimagined through modern eyes.
