MODEL LAMINA

LAMINA by Officine Allegra exemplifies the essence of Italian engineering—where elegant design meets uncompromising performance. Built for today’s baristas, Lamina offers a seamless blend of style, functionality, and cutting-edge features tailored for the demands of high-paced coffee environments.

Precision and consistency are at the heart of LAMINA’s design. With its robust 11.4-liter boiler system, this machine maintains stable temperatures and steam power throughout service. The dual-group configuration ensures simultaneous espresso extraction, allowing efficient operation during busy hours without compromising on quality.

Operating at 3000W / 4000W with a 230-240V 50Hz power setup, LAMINA delivers powerful performance and energy efficiency. Whether in a boutique café or a bustling coffee house, its sturdy construction and high-grade components provide long-lasting reliability and ease of use for professionals.

Refined yet bold, LAMINA features an ergonomic layout and is crafted with premium materials that elevate any coffee station. With optional custom finishes and sleek modern lines, it’s more than just a machine—it’s a centerpiece of the coffee experience. Discover precision brewing the Italian way with LAMINA.
