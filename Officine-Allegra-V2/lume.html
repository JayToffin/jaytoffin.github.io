01.Engineered Features

Built for
the ritual.

Six features, six gestures — every component engineered to serve the barista's craft without getting in the way.

LUME
  1. 01

    Quick-Opening Steam Valve

    — fast-response knob, instant steam delivery.

    Refined quick-opening knob tap for safe, fast steam control — designed for effortless milk texturing in continuous service.

  2. 02

    Cool-Touch Steam Wand

    — safe to touch, performant in use.

    Insulated steam wand stays cool to the touch — safety without sacrificing milk-texturing performance. Crafted for continuous use without overheating.

  3. 03

    Timer · Seconds Counter

    — Automatic variant only.

    Built-in extraction timer with a seconds counter on the Automatic variant — set the target, hit it consistently. Every shot becomes measurable.

  4. 04

    Automatic Group Cleaning

    — Automatic variant exclusive.

    The Automatic variant handles backflush cleaning cycles on schedule — fewer manual steps, more consistent hygiene, longer service life for every component downstream.

  5. 05

    Programmable Water Delivery

    — set, save, repeat — across both variants.

    Both Lume variants ship with a programmable water button — dial in your shot volume once and reproduce it all-day long. Consistency in routine, freedom to tune when needed.

  6. 06

    Integrated Work-Area Light

    — light where it matters, on both variants.

    A built-in work-area light illuminates the group and portafilter — clear visibility for shot assessment, milk texturing, and routine cleaning.
