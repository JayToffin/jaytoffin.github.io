Italy Operation

TFN ITALIA SRL

Toffin Italia Srl serves as the holding company for our manufacturing operations in Italy, with a primary focus on the design and production of high-quality espresso machines under our proprietary brands. This is where Italian craftsmanship meets our vision for innovation in coffee technology.


Our long-term vision for Toffin Italia is to establish a multi-brand manufacturing group—home to a portfolio of espresso machine and grinder brands that combine engineering excellence, design heritage, and modern functionality. Through strategic investment, talent acquisition, and design innovation, Toffin Italia aims to become a leading force in the global coffee equipment industry.

Business units:

Officine Allegra Srl

Located in Milan, Italy, Officine Allegra Srl is the dedicated factory for the development, manufacturing, and sales of mass-market espresso machines. The company focuses on combining Italian engineering excellence with accessible, performance-driven design.


Officine Allegra Srl operates under two main brand lines:

  • Officine Allegra
  • Studio OFA

Together, these lines reflect our vision to bring high-quality, Italian-made espresso machines to a wider global audience—without compromising on authenticity, durability, or design.

Main Brand

Officine Allegra

crafted with precision, purpose, and a deep respect for the essentials. Designed for the demands of the mass market, Officine Allegra focuses on what truly matters behind the bar: reliability, consistency, ease of use, and enduring performance.

Every component is built to last. Every line is drawn with intention. The result is a machine that performs flawlessly in high-pressure environments, day after day. With a clean, confident design language and a mechanically driven soul, Officine Allegra is a true workhorse—quietly powerful, unshakably stable, and intuitively responsive.

This is not a machine that tries to do everything.

Studio OFA

A prosumer range designed for passionate home baristas and small-scale professionals. Studio OFA brings commercial-grade brewing capability into a compact form factor—offering performance, reliability, and a truly Italian coffee experience for homes, boutique cafés, and design-conscious spaces.

Each machine is thoughtfully crafted with beautiful, minimalist design that blends seamlessly into modern interiors—whether it’s a curated kitchen, a stylish café counter, or a creative studio. Studio OFA is where precision engineering meets timeless aesthetics, for those who value both form and function in their daily ritual.
