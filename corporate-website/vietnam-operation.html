Vietnam Operation

Toffin.vn

CÔNG TY TNHH TOFFIN VIETNAM

Toffin Vietnam operates with the same business model and commitment as Toffin Indonesia—focused on the distribution of premium coffee machines, F&B equipment, and high-quality beverage ingredients. We serve the HORECA sector (hotels, restaurants, and cafés), providing our partners with the tools, products, and support they need to grow.


While the structure and services mirror those of Toffin.id, the key difference lies in our specialized brand portfolio tailored to the Vietnamese market. Our current distributed brands include:

  • Officine Allegra
  • Rocket Espresso
  • VBM (Vibiemme)

Through Toffin Vietnam, we are laying the foundation for a stronger presence in Southeast Asia—bringing world-class products, technical expertise, and dependable service to one of the region’s fastest-growing coffee and hospitality markets.
