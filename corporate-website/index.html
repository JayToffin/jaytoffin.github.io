About Us

Toffin began its journey in 2007 from a small bedroom with just one employee and a mission: to bring premium beverage ingredients from America to Indonesia.

From these humble beginnings, Toffin has grown into a powerhouse of over 800 employees, with branch offices and distribution networks across 20 major cities in Indonesia.


Today, Toffin has evolved into Toffin Group, an integrated business ecosystem operating under Toffin Kapital, the holding entity for our group’s ventures in Indonesia and abroad.

Our operations span across B2B and D2C distribution, digital platforms, manufacturing, and international expansion.


Toffin Group aims to redefine our business model by combining proprietary intellectual property, multi-point production capabilities, and a diversified distribution network.

This model is designed to balance control with scalability, ensuring we uphold the highest quality standards while accelerating our expansion into high-growth international markets.
