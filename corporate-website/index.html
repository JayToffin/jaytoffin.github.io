About Us

From a small bedroom in 2007, with just one employee and a simple goal, to bring beverage ingredients from the USA to Indonesia during the rise of the coffee shop trend. Toffin began its journey.


What started as a modest operation has steadily grown into a leading player in the coffee and beverage industry. Today, Toffin operates in multiple countries, branch office in Vietnam, a presence in 29 major cities across Indonesia, and manufacturing facility in Italy. This evolution has evolved Toffin into Toffin Group, an integrated business under Toffin Kapital, driving ventures that span B2B and D2C distribution, digital platforms, manufacturing, and international expansion.


Building on this foundation, our aim at Toffin Group is to integrate proprietary intellectual property, multi-point production capabilities, and a robust distribution network to create a business model that is scalable, resilient, and future-ready — all while upholding our commitment to uncompromising quality and accelerating our global growth.


We continue to grow with purpose, guided by innovation, collaboration, and a drive to deliver lasting value to our partners and customers.
