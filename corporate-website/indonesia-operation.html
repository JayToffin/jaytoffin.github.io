PT Toffin Indonesia

As the heart of the group, PT Toffin Indonesia is the leading distributor of coffee machines, F&B equipment, and premium beverage ingredients. Serving the HORECA (hotel, restaurant, café) sector, Toffin has become the trusted partner of thousands of businesses across the country.


With a portfolio of over 1,000 SKUs from both international and local premium brands, we offer more than products—we deliver complete solutions. From consultation and installation to barista training and after-sales service, our mission is to empower our customers to grow through quality, innovation, and long-term partnership.

PT Toffin Digital

PT Toffin Digital was originally conceived to migrate our customer interactions from physical to digital. With a wide product range and a large customer base, we saw the need to create a platform where customers could easily explore Toffin’s offerings—spanning over 1,000 SKUs—and where we could better understand our customers’ behaviors and preferences.


Over time, this entity has evolved into our technology and innovation hub. From building our own ERP and mobile app, to developing AI-powered tools for smarter business operations, Toffin Digital is now at the forefront of our transformation. It plays a central role in positioning Toffin not just as a distributor—but as a tech-driven company operating in the F&B space.

PT Toffin Casa Indonesia

PT Toffin Casa Indonesia is focused on the distribution of high-end home appliances, primarily the iconic Italian brand SMEG.


With a focus on design, innovation, and lifestyle, Toffin Casa brings European aesthetics and functionality into modern homes across Indonesia. We don’t just sell kitchen appliances—we deliver aspirational living experiences that reflect taste, quality, and timeless style.
